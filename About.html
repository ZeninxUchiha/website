Bulawayo Nursing Care Agency was formed in 1996 after the realization that the healthcare sector can be overwhelmed with patients and at the same time those patients still needed nursing care after being discharged from the hospital. Hence the a need for training high-quality nurse assistants and rst aiders as such the nurse assistant training school and nursing agency both complement each other ie the school trains and grooms nurses who will be able to cover the nursing agency which renders private care or home nursing services to a wide variety of clientele.
